# 🕵️ Assignment 01: AI Detective Mode

## Objective

Explore how Artificial Intelligence is being used in the real world and develop critical thinking about AI technologies.

---

## Choose One Level

### 🟢 Beginner – AI Spotter

List 5 AI-powered products or services you used this week.

For each:

- Product Name
- AI Feature
- Data Used
- One sentence explanation

**Deliverable:** 1-page report

---

### 🔵 Intermediate – AGI Debate

Research:

> Will AGI benefit humanity or create risks?

Tasks:

- Read one article
- Summarize both viewpoints
- Include your opinion
- Cite sources

**Deliverable:** 200–300 word summary

---

### 🟠 Advanced – Family Interview

Interview a non-technical family member.

Questions:

- What is AI?
- Have you used AI?
- What benefits do you see?
- What concerns do you have?

Compare their understanding with what you learned in Week 1.

**Deliverable:** 1-page reflection
