# 📚 Topics Covered

## 1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples include:

* Face recognition
* Language translation
* Recommendation systems
* Voice assistants
* Chatbots

### Key Insight

> AI is pattern recognition at scale. It is not consciousness, magic, or human-like thinking.

---

## 2. AI vs Machine Learning vs Deep Learning

### Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The broad field of creating intelligent systems.

Examples:

* Rule-based chatbots
* Expert systems
* Recommendation systems

### Machine Learning (ML)

A subset of AI where systems learn from data rather than being explicitly programmed.

Examples:

* Spam detection
* Product recommendations
* Fraud detection

### Deep Learning (DL)

A subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks with many layers.

Examples:

* ChatGPT
* Image generation models
* Speech recognition systems

### Relationship

```text
AI
 └── Machine Learning
      └── Deep Learning
```

Remember:

> AI ⊃ ML ⊃ DL

---

## 3. Types of AI

### Narrow AI (ANI)

Designed to perform a specific task.

Examples:

* Google Translate
* Spotify Recommendations
* Face ID
* ChatGPT

Most AI systems today belong to this category.

---

### Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

A theoretical AI capable of performing any intellectual task that a human can perform.

Characteristics:

* Learns across domains
* Adapts to new situations
* Human-level intelligence

AGI does not yet exist.

---

### Super AI (ASI)

A hypothetical form of AI that surpasses human intelligence in every domain.

Currently remains theoretical.

## 6. Structured vs Unstructured Data

### Structured Data

Organized in rows and columns.

Examples:

* Excel files
* SQL databases
* CSV files

### Unstructured Data

Not organized in a fixed format.

Examples:

* Images
* Videos
* Audio

Approximately 80% of the world's data is unstructured.

## 7. Logic and Algorithmic Thinking

Computers make decisions using logical conditions.

### Example: Smart Fridge

```text
IF milk_amount < 1 liter
    THEN add milk to grocery list

IF egg_expiry < 2 days
    THEN notify user

IF door_open > 60 seconds
    THEN sound alarm
```

### Example: Smart Traffic Light

```text
IF traffic_count > 50
    THEN extend green light

IF ambulance_detected
    THEN turn light green

IF no_vehicle_detected
    THEN reduce wait time
```

### Example: Smart Classroom

```text
IF attendance < 75%
    THEN send warning

IF assignment_missing
    THEN notify student

IF score > 90
    THEN award certificate
```

# 🚀 Recent AI Advancements (2024–2026)

## 1. Multimodal AI

AI systems can understand:

* Text
* Images
* Audio
* Video

Examples:

* GPT-5
* Claude
* Gemini
* Qwen-VL

---

## 2. Agentic AI

AI systems capable of taking actions on behalf of users.

Examples:

* Scheduling tasks
* Running code
* Sending emails
* Booking services

---

## 3. Reasoning Models

AI systems that perform step-by-step reasoning before answering.

Examples:

* OpenAI o-series
* DeepSeek-R1
* Gemini Thinking
* Claude Extended Thinking

---

## 4. Open Source AI

Open-source models are rapidly improving.

Examples:

* Llama
* DeepSeek
* Qwen
* Mistral
* GLM

Today, powerful AI models can run on personal computers.
